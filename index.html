Hi, my name is Art (I know - great name right?) and I'm an art student from Berkshire, UK.

I love to sketch, paint and create art with many mediums.

Services

I will take a photo and create a pencil sketch to create a unique piece of art to show off your favourite pet.

Take a photo

Capture your pet in a portrait photo. If you can take a few options we can pick the one that will work best in a sketch

Request your sketch

Fill in the form (see below) to request your sketch. I will email you to let you know when I've received your request.

I will sketch your pet

I will let know know how long it will take to create you sketch and send you a preview photo

I will post your sketch

Once you are happy with your preview I will send your picture by registered post. I currently only send to UK addresses
